Risotto Recipe

Many have asked for my Risotto recipe so here it is,
In ALL its glory!

Ingredients

  • Risotto rice
  • Rice vinegar
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil half a litre of water in a large pot
  2. Add the risotto rice
  3. Cook for 15 Minutes
    15:00
  4. Add a teaspoon of rice vinegar and a dash of salt
  5. Finish with salt and pepper
